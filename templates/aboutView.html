ABOUT ADAM

Owned and operated by Adam Aquino, integrity and top-notch customer service is AA Motorworks highest priority. For the past 8 years Adam has been a Professional Automotive Technician, starting his career at a Subaru specialty shop, he then branched out to other Japanese & European automotive shops to broaden his skill-set. Adam also is a 10 year military veteran of the U.S. Air Force and served in the U.S. Air Force Reserves as an Avionics Technician. With Adam's vast knowledge, troubleshooting skills, and sharp attention to detail, you will not be let down. Adam opened AA Motorworks in 2014 to provide a trustworthy and personal automotive service and repair experience for the greater Silicon Valley.
