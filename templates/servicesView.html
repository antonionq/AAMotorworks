Compression/Leak Down Test

A compression test will tell you your compression for each cylinder. If your car is losing a significant amount of oil, we recommend that you come in for a test to determine the health of your motor.

A leak down test works the opposite way. Instead of measuring a cylinder's ability to make pressure, it measures pressure loss.
