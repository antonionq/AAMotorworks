Forester XTi

You might remember the 412whp/435ftlb 2006 STi we built not too long ago. It had an AA Stg2 long block, Blouch Dom 1.5XT-R turbo, 1300cc Injector Dynamics squirters, ran on E85, etc.

Well it had a meeting with a freeway jersey barrier. Luckily we were able to salvage most of the parts and swap it over into the customers newly purchased 2004 Forester XT

We also fitted the Forester with new lifted suspension using King Springs, KYB struts and Whiteline Sway Bars. Wheels and tires are Method Rally MR502's with Yokohama A/T rubbers.

  • BlouchTurbo Dom 1.5
  • GrimmSpeed TMIC
  • COBB Tuning Intake
  • AA Motorworks Built Longblock

    • 405 WTQE85: 405 WHP
