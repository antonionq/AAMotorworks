Subaru Modification Stages

Stage 1

Are you looking for a good little bump in power without having to make any huge physical modifications to your Subaru? Stage 1 generally consists of just an ECU reflash where boost pressure is raised. It’s common for this stage to also include an intake. Stage 1 can be achieved via Cobb’s Accessport or protune.

Stage 2

The stock exhausts from Subaru can be fairly restrictive. This is where we enter the most common performance upgrade with Subarus. Stage 2 consists of replacing your stock exhaust with an aftermarket turboback 3-4" exhaust system. This will allow for a significant increase of airflow compared to the stock system. Along with the increase of power, your car will also be noticeably louder. Just like Stage 1, Stage 2 can be flashed via Cobb's Accessport or can be setup through a protune.
